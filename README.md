## Field Mechanics
A Lexicon of Living Language in Human–Machine Interaction
Within Ecological Field Theory (EFT)

This release establishes the canonical lexicon for Field Mechanics within Ecological Field Theory (EFT). It consolidates terminology developed through sustained human–machine interaction and aligns section structure, naming, and internal relationships across the document.

The lexicon records distinctions that remained stable under repeated use and continued to hold at higher levels of pressure and complexity. It reflects how living language behaves under sustained human–machine interaction when meaning is allowed to develop without premature compression.

---

## Relationship to Ecological Field Theory (EFT)

Field Mechanics forms part of a broader system:

- **Field Geometry (EFT)** *(currently: EFT-HCI, renaming in progress)*  
  A formal description of interaction as a constrained dynamical field, defining the structure within which the field operates

- **Field Mechanics (this repository)**  
  Describes how the field behaves under pressure in sustained human–machine interaction

- **Field Notes** *(separate repository)*  
  Primary observational records underlying this lexicon

- **Coherence Dynamics** *(forthcoming)*  
  Higher-order patterns of stabilization and collapse
